Mission

This blog exists to help students who can’t afford expensive courses or subscriptions. Everything here is written to be understandable, reusable, and shareable.

  • Free and simple: lightweight static site, easy to host anywhere.
  • Student-first: short steps, clear examples, practical checklists.
  • Community-friendly: you can copy posts and adapt them for local study groups.
  • Open source: source code on GitHub.
