This blog shares free learning materials related to Claude Code—especially for students in under-developed and developing nations who can’t afford expensive courses or subscriptions.

If you can read this page, you can copy it, share it, and improve it for your community.

What you’ll find here

  • Beginner guides that start from zero and avoid jargon.
  • Workflows and checklists you can reuse while practicing.
  • Small project templates designed to be easy to translate and share.

How to use this blog

  1. Start with “Claude Code CLI basics”.
  2. Then read “Free materials” and copy the templates.
  3. Pick one small topic per week (Git, JavaScript basics, Python basics, etc.).
  4. Practice by building tiny projects and writing short notes.
  5. Share your improved notes locally—study groups make learning faster.

Respectful and responsible use

Please follow official terms of service, your school rules, and local laws. This project is educational and aims to promote fair access—not misuse.
